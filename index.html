Industrial Weighing Equipment Supplier in Kenya

Microworld Africa is an organization that develops,designs and manufactures weighing, measuring and automation equipment used in laboratories and industries.

We are dedicated to helping your business operations within Africa and beyond through the provision of efficient services aimed at increasing the productivity of our clients.

The company is committed to delivering high-precision instruments that enhance operational efficiency, ensure data accuracy, and meet global quality standards.

Microworld Africa serves sectors such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, agriculture, manufacturing, and research institutions across Africa.

Based in Jekima Centre opposite St. Stephen's Church, Jogoo Road, Nairobi, Kenya, Microworld Africa provides its services locally and internationally, not only in terms of products but also in sales and after-sale services.
